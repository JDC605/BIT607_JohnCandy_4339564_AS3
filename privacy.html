Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Pet details
  • Interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Make customer bookings
  • To keep in contact about deals and recent promotions
  • Collect analytical information of website use, to improve the user experience

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter personal details and contact information, we'll be unable to complete your booking online. You will need to contact Snazzy Paws Grooming using Contact Form or by phoning 07 111 3344.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allowing admin staff to view it.
